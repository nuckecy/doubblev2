What is Doubble?

Doubble is an automated investment platform that allows you invest at your convenience and get back up to 200% over a period.

With Doubble, you choose to invest either in one contribution (lump sum) or monthly contributions – which could be for the duration of 3 years (36 months) or 5 years (60 months). All payouts are paid monthly in equal installments to you or any beneficiary of your choice.

Ready to watch your money grow? Choose your variant and let us do the magic.

Benefits of Doubble

  • Enables you get loan facilities against your Doubble balance.
  • Provides the option of choosing a different beneficiary other than self (e.g. Spouse, Kid, Parents).
  • Empowers you to plan towards future consistent cash outflows such as payment for utilities, monthly upkeep & payment for mortgages.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pre-terminate my Investment?

Yes. The investment plan can be pre-terminated. However, if terminated before the end of the tenor, you will get your full contributions forfeiting 50% of the accrued earnings.

How do I know my balance?

You can view your balance on the platform using your log-in details.

Can I purchase multiple plans?

Yes, you can purchase multiple plans for your benefit or other beneficiaries.

Can I opt for a lump sum payout instead of monthly payout?

With Doubble, this is not possible as the plans are designed to pay out monthly over time to address future cashflow sustainability challenge. However, if due to an emergency, you need to collect the contribution in lump sum, the plan will be terminated. You will get your full contributions but will forfeit 50% of the accrued earnings.

Is the beneficiary transferable?

Yes, you can select or nominate a new beneficiary for the pay-out period.

Can I just transfer any loose cash into my Doubble plan?

No, the monthly contribution is automated from the start date of the plan. We recommend you start a new plan.

What is the minimum monthly contribution amount?

The minimum monthly contribution amount is N5,000.

What is the minimum lump sum amount?

The minimum lump sum amount is N100,000.

What is the maximum amount to contribute on Doubble?

We are glad to say, there is no maximum amount to invest on Doubble.

When will my account be debited and my Doubble Account funded?

This will be on the day the first contribution was made for every month.   Example: If your first contribution was made August 30th, your account will be debited and funded on the 30th of every month.

When do I get my monthly payout from the Bank?

Payouts are credited on the same date the first contribution was made each month. 
Example: If your Doubble account was funded on the 30th day of every month, your payouts will also be on the 30th of every month.

How do I fund my account?

Funding your Doubble account is automated.

If you are a Sterling Bank customer, you simply state the account to be debited.

If you are not a Sterling Bank customer, you will have to transfer funds into your new Sterling Bank account opened for you. This account will then be debited automatically on the expected date of contribution.

How long is my savings period before I start getting my payouts?

This depends on the Doubble Investment plan selected. Click here to read the options available.

How can I know my expected earnings before investing?

We have built a Calculator that shows all the information you need. Try it by clicking here.

Is Doubble open to only customers of Sterling Bank?

No, Doubble is open to everyone. While applying, an account will be opened for you and your account details sent to your email immediately to enable you invest.

Can I choose a future start date when I apply now?

Yes, you can apply now and choose a convenient start date.
